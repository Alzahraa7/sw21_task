Overview

STP was founded in 2006 at Cairo university;
The purpose of STP is to strengthen the leadership, skills, effectiveness and efficiency of involved people;
enabling them to further enrich the quality of the community and their personal life.

Mission

To be a Leading organization working on developing youth who will lead positive changes.

Vission

To develop youth in various fields personally and technically through academic programs,
projects, and events in a values oriented system, to take our part in providing a generation capable of playing its roles effictively towards itself,
community and country.
